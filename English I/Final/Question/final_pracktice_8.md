## **3(b) Essay (8 – Sample: Topic 2)**

### *The Importance of Water Conservation*

Water is one of the most valuable natural resources on earth. However, due to population growth and climate change, water scarcity is becoming a serious global problem.

Water conservation helps ensure the availability of clean water for future generations. Simple practices like rainwater harvesting, reducing wastage, and reusing water can make a big difference. Conservation also protects the environment and maintains ecological balance.

In conclusion, water conservation is essential for sustainable development. Everyone should be aware of their responsibility to save water and use it wisely.
