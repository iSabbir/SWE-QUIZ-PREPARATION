## Part C: Grammar

### **3. a)** Write the correct form of the verbs in the brackets to complete the following conditional sentences.

**[6×0.5 = 3]**
i. If you (heat) ______ ice, it melts. *(Zero Conditional)*

ii. If I (be) ______ taller, I would join the basketball team. *(Second Conditional)*

iii. If she (wake) ______ up early, she will catch the bus. *(First Conditional)*

iv. If they had followed the rules, they (not face) ______ punishment. *(Third Conditional)*

v. I (help) ______ you if you ask me politely. *(First Conditional)*

vi. If he were more careful, he (avoid) ______ mistakes. *(Second Conditional)*

---

### **3. b)** Identify if the following sentences are **Fragment, Run-on, or Complete**.

If Fragment or Run-on, rewrite correctly.

**[7×1 = 7]**
i. Because the class was canceled.

ii. I finished my homework I went to sleep.

iii. She enjoys reading books.

iv. Walking alone in the dark street.

v. The teacher explained the lesson students listened carefully.

vi. After the storm ended.

vii. He was tired, but he continued working.

---

## ✅ **Answers with Bangla Explanation**

### **3(a) Answers**
i. **heat**

ii. **were**

iii. **wakes**

iv. **would not have faced**

v. **will help**

vi. **would avoid**

### **Bangla Explanation (Conditionals)**

* **Zero Conditional:** If + present → present (scientific truth)

* **First Conditional:** If + present → will + verb (real future)

* **Second Conditional:** If + past → would + verb (imaginary now)

* **Third Conditional:** If + had + V3 → would have + V3 (past regret)

---

### **3(b) Answers**
i. **Fragment** → Because the class was canceled, we went home.

ii. **Run-on** → I finished my homework, so I went to sleep.

iii. **Complete Sentence**

iv. **Fragment** → I was walking alone in the dark street.

v. **Run-on** → The teacher explained the lesson, and students listened carefully.

vi. **Fragment** → After the storm ended, people went outside.

vii. **Complete Sentence**
