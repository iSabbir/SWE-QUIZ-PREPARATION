# Topic 01: Lines of Code (LOC) - Online Course Enrollment & Learning System

An Online Course Enrollment and E-Learning System is being developed for an educational institution. Students can browse courses, enroll, watch lectures, download study materials, and submit assignments. Before enrollment, the system must validate student eligibility, check seat availability, verify payment status. After enrollment, the system gives access to video lectures, tracks progress, collects assignments and generates student performance reports.

## Major modules identified:

1. Course Browsing & Enrollment Module  
2. Learning Management (Lectures + Materials + Progress)  
3. Assignment & Performance Reporting Module
