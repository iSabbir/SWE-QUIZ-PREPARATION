## **1. Water Pollution (‡¶ú‡¶≤ ‡¶¶‡ßÇ‡¶∑‡¶£)**

Water becomes polluted when harmful chemicals, wastes, and germs mix with it.

**Simple reasons:**

* Factories throw dirty chemical waste into rivers.
* Cities release sewage (‡¶Æ‡¶≤‡¶Æ‡ßÇ‡¶§‡ßç‡¶∞) without treatment.
* Fertilizers and pesticides from farms mix with water.
* Arsenic contamination in tube wells.
* Bad well construction causes fecal contamination (‡¶Æ‡¶≤‡¶¶‡ßÇ‡¶∑‡¶£).

**Example:**
Buriganga and Shitalakhya are the most polluted rivers in Bangladesh.

## **2. Air Pollution (‡¶¨‡¶æ‡¶Ø‡¶º‡ßÅ ‡¶¶‡ßÇ‡¶∑‡¶£)**

Air becomes dirty and harmful to breathe.

**Simple reasons:**

* Old and unfit vehicles release smoke.
* Brick kilns produce toxic gases.
* Construction work releases dust.
* Burning of biomass and waste.

**Effect:**
About **15,000 people die early every year** because of air pollution.

## **3. Noise Pollution (‡¶∂‡¶¨‡ßç‡¶¶ ‡¶¶‡ßÇ‡¶∑‡¶£)**

Too much unwanted sound that harms our ears and health.

**Simple reasons:**

* Traffic horns
* Loud engines of buses and trucks
* Construction activities
* Industrial machines

Noise in many areas of Dhaka is much higher than the safety limit.

---

## **4. River Pollution & Encroachment (‡¶®‡¶¶‡ßÄ ‡¶¶‡ßÇ‡¶∑‡¶£ ‡¶ì ‡¶¶‡¶ñ‡¶≤)**

Rivers become polluted, narrow, and weak.

**Reasons:**

* Factories dump waste directly into rivers.
* Human waste is thrown into rivers.
* Illegal river grabbing (‡¶®‡¶¶‡ßÄ ‡¶¶‡¶ñ‡¶≤) reduces water flow.
* Ships and launches throw oil and garbage.

**Example:**

**Reasons:**

* Deforestation
* Pollution
* Over-hunting
* Destroying natural habitats
* One-crop plantations reduce plant variety

## **6. Soil Erosion (‡¶Æ‡¶æ‡¶ü‡¶ø ‡¶ï‡ßç‡¶∑‡¶Ø‡¶º)**

**Reasons:**

* Cutting hills
* Deforestation
* Heavy rain
* Flash floods

**Effects:**

* Land loses fertility
* Rivers become silted
* Flood increases

## **8. Deforestation (‡¶¨‡¶® ‡¶â‡¶ú‡¶æ‡¶°‡¶º)**

**Reasons:**

* Illegal logging
* Using wood as fuel
* Expanding agriculture

## **1. Stockholm Conference 1972**

* First major global environmental conference.
* Created **UNEP (United Nations Environment Programme)**.

---

## **2. UNEP (UN Environment Programme)**

* Protects global environment.
* Runs tree-planting campaigns.
* Helps countries reduce pollution.

* Provides scientific reports on climate change.
* Won the Nobel Peace Prize.

* Agreement to reduce greenhouse gas emissions.
* Introduced **CDM (Clean Development Mechanism)**.
* Developed countries invest in developing countries to reduce emissions.

---

## **5. UNFCCC & COP Conferences**

* Annual climate meetings of the world.
* COP21 Paris Agreement: keep warming below 2¬∞C.

---

## **6. IUCN & Greenpeace**

* IUCN protects biodiversity.
* Greenpeace promotes renewable energy.
